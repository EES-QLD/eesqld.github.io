Who We Are

Practical Engineering.
Certifiable Results.

Engineering & Equipment Services QLD (EES QLD) is a Mackay-based mechanical engineering consultancy delivering practical, compliant, and certifiable engineering solutions to the mining, transport, lifting, and heavy industry sectors across Queensland.

Principal engineer Andrew Mitchell holds CPEng and RPEQ credentials with over 20 years of hands-on experience across mining, coal terminals, ports, and heavy industrial operations — from procurement and engineering through to site execution and sign-off.

EES QLD provides engineering authority and RPEQ sign-off as a single accountable point of contact. No hand-offs. No junior-only delivery.
